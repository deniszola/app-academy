# Binary Practice

In this exercise, translate the following values into the appropriate number base

## Binary to base 10

1. 0b1010 = 10
1. 0b0011 = 3

## Binary to hexadecimal

1. 0b1010 = 0xA
1. 0b0011 = 0x3

## Hexadecimal to base 10

1. 0xa1 = 161
1. 0xff = 255

## Hexadecimal to binary

1. 0xa1 = 1010 0001
1. 0xff = 1111 1111

## Base 10 to binary

1. 8 = 1000
1. 24 = 0001 1000
1. 255 = 1111 1111

## Base 10 to hexadecimal

1. 8 = 0x8
1. 24 = 0x18
1. 255 = 0xFF

## Base 10 to ASCII

1. 65 = A
1. 66 = B
1. 97 = a
1. 98 = b

## Hexadecimal to ASCII

1. 0x41 = A
1. 0x42 = B
1. 0x61 = a
1. 0x62 = b

## Binary to ASCII

1. 0b01000001 = A
1. 0b01000010 = B
1. 0b01100001 = a
1. 0b01100010 = b
